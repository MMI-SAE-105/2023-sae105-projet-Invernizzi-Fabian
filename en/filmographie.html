Filmography

Published on 25/10, by Fabian Invernizzi, Reading time: 3 min

Christopher Nolan has not only directed "Oppenheimer." During his career, he has been able to direct films that have achieved tremendous success, such as :

The Dark Knight

2008 – Action / Thriller

Summary : In Gotham City, a city plagued by violence, crime, and corruption, a group of armed robbers rob a bank. Each must dispose of their accomplices until only one remains: the mastermind of the heist, the Joker, a psychopathic, childish, and cruel clown.

Main actors : Christian Bale / Heath Ledger / Gary Oldman / Michael Caine / Aaron Eckhart…

Interstellar

2014 – Science-Fiction

Summary : As Earth is dying, a team of astronauts travels through a wormhole that has appeared near Saturn, leading to another galaxy. Their mission is to explore a new star system in the hope of finding a habitable planet and establishing a space colony to save humanity.

Main actors : Matthew McConauGhey / Anne Hathaway / Michael Caine / Jessica Chastain…

Inception

2010 – Science-Fiction

Summary : Dom Cobb is an experienced thief – the best in the dangerous art of extraction: his specialty is to steal the most precious secrets of an individual, hidden deep in their subconscious, while they are dreaming and their mind is particularly vulnerable.

Main actors : Léonardo DiCaprio / Joseph Gordon-Levitt / Cillian Murphy / Tom Hardy…

Dunkerque

2017 – History / War

Summary : Between May 26 and June 4, 1940, the beaches of Dunkirk were ravaged by war. Allied soldiers from Belgium, England, Canada, and France were caught under fire from German forces. A massive evacuation operation was launched in an attempt to save them.

Main actors : Harry Styles / Fionn Whitehead / Cillian Murphy / Tom Hardy…

Tenet

2020 – Science-Fiction / Action

Summary : A CIA agent, the "protagonist" (John David Washington), infiltrates a Russian covert operation: the theft of a mysterious object, thought to be plutonium, during a hostage situation in an opera in Kiev. His mission will project him into a dimension that transcends time. However, this is not a time travel, but a temporal reversal.

Main actors : Elizabeth Debicki / Jhon David Washington / Robert Pattinson…
