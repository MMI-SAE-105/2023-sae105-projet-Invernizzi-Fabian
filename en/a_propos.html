Who I am ?

Hello, my name is Fabian Invernizzi, I am currently in the 1st year of study in the MMI (Multimedia and Internet Professions) training located in Montbéliard.

Objective ?

This website is a study project that aims to present a movie quote as well as different articles on similar topics.

Why this quote ?

I chose the quote "Now I am become Death, the destroyer of worlds" because, first of all, it has many hidden meanings. In addition, spoken by Cillian Murphy, it is really very intriguing and spectacular.
